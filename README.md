# 90's Car Game 🚗

> A large-scale open-world driving experience built from the ground up in Unity — currently in active development.

## 🌍 Overview

An open-world driving game set across a massive **12km × 12km island** — one of the largest maps built in Unity by an indie developer. The world features a full highway and road network, a city with buildings under active construction, and vast open terrain to explore. Originally built in Unity URP and recently migrated to **Unity HDRP** for significantly improved visuals, real-time water rendering, and a higher-fidelity lighting pipeline.

## 🎮 Live Demo

> ⚠️ This WebGL demo is from February 2026 (URP build). The project has since been fully migrated to HDRP with major improvements — a lot more is coming.

👉 **[Play the Demo](https://rayhasgor07.github.io/90-s-Car-game/)**

## ✨ Features

- **Massive open world** — 12km × 12km explorable island with highways, roads, and a developing city
- **Realistic vehicle physics** — custom-built handling and driving feel from scratch in C#
- **Speedometer UI** — real-time speed display tied to vehicle physics
- **Minimap** — live minimap tracking player position across the world
- **Realistic car sounds** — engine audio that responds dynamically to speed and acceleration
- **HDRP pipeline** — real-time water, high-fidelity lighting, and physically-based materials (post-URP migration)
- **Hand-crafted road network** — highways and city roads built manually across the entire map
- **City environment** — buildings and urban areas currently in active development

## 🛠️ Built With

- **Unity HDRP** (migrated from URP)
- **C#** — all gameplay systems, vehicle physics, UI
- **Blender** — 3D vehicle and environment modeling

## 🚧 Status

Actively in development. The demo linked above reflects an early URP build. Current development is focused on:
- Expanding the city and adding more buildings
- Polishing HDRP lighting and materials
- Additional vehicles and gameplay systems
